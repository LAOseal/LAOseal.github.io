Wavefront Affectors

Classes for all devices that have the capability to affect the wavefront:

  • Deformable mirrors (ALPAO97 and MEMS648)

  • Spatial Light Modulator (Meadowlark)

  • Segemented mirror (IRISAO)

  • Fast steering mirror (PI)

Warning

  • Check humidity on MEMS Deformable mirror before starting it (windows machine)

  • Don’t forget to shutdown DMs when work is over

Note

Pupil footprints on DMs is smaller than clear apertures, leading to somee unseen actuators (especially for MEMS DM).
